The Major Dynasties

Han Dynasty

206 BCE – 220 CE

Often considered a golden age, the Han Dynasty established Confucianism as the state ideology and opened the Silk Road, connecting China to the West.

Media from the Han Dynasty

Tang Dynasty

618 – 907 CE

A period of high culture, flourishing arts, and religious tolerance.

Song Dynasty

960 – 1279 CE

The Song Dynasty was a time of incredible technological innovation.

Ming Dynasty

1368 – 1644 CE

Known for its architectural achievements like the Forbidden City.

Qing Dynasty

1644 – 1912 CE

The final imperial dynasty of China.
